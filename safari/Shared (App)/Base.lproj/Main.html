Comments Owl for Hacker News is enabled in Safari

To enable Comments Owl for Hacker News in Safari:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap Apps → Safari → Extensions
  3. Tap Comments Owl for Hacker News
  4. Tap Allow Extension
  5. Scroll down to Permissions
  6. Tap news.ycombinator.com then Allow

To Enable Comments Owl for Hacker News in Safari:

  1. Enable Comments Owl for Hacker News
  2. Click Edit Websites…
  3. Change news.ycombinator.com to Allow
